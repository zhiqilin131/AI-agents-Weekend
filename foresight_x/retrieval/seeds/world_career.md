# Career decision cache (demo)

When comparing two internship or job offers, separate **decision quality** from **identity comfort**.
A strong process lists unknowns (manager access, tech stack, conversion rate to full-time) and assigns each a data-gathering action before the deadline.

Negotiation norms: requesting a short extension to finish another process is standard in many campus recruiting programs; employers may say no, but asking is rarely a permanent burn if done professionally.

If one option is time-bound and the other is not yet realized (e.g., a future interview), the dominant uncertainty is information value: quantify what you learn in the next 48–72 hours versus the cost of losing the bird-in-hand offer.
